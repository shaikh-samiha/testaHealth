About Testa Center

Testa Center is an open and friendly testbed for biological production, leveraging state-of-the-art bioprocessing labs, equipment, and expertise to drive health technology innovations.

We provide an authentic production environment where innovators can develop,
test, and validate their healthcare solutions in a setting that mirrors real-world conditions.

We bring together scientists, clinicians, engineers, and digital health experts to create better solutions, faster—because great ideas grow stronger through teamwork.

Every project we support is driven by one goal: to improve patient care through high-quality, accessible, and smart digital health technologies.
